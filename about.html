Who We Are

SMR Enterprises is a Vijayawada-based supplier of construction and building materials. Founded and managed by Mahamad Rafi, we have built a strong reputation for providing quality products at competitive prices.

We supply a comprehensive range of building materials including premium paints from leading brands, all types of cement, pipes and fittings, sanitary products, and professional pest control services.
